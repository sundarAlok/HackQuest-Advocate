Bug Report – Temporary Email Detection Failure

Reported On: 02/01/2026

Open HackQuest Platform

Bug Summary

HackQuest currently allows users to successfully register and become verified platform users using temporary / disposable email services.

  • The system does not block known temp-mail domains.
  • Accounts created using disposable emails gain full access.
  • No warning or restriction is applied during signup or verification.

This behavior enables users to bypass identity reliability checks, potentially leading to spam, abuse, and fake user inflation.

Criticality & Impact

High Severity Security / Trust Issue Authentication & Validation

Why This Is a Problem

  • Allows creation of multiple fake or throwaway accounts.
  • Weakens platform trust and referral integrity.
  • Can be abused for farming rewards and incentives.
  • Undermines user authenticity and community quality.

Steps to Reproduce

  1. Open a temporary email service (e.g., disposable inbox).
  2. Sign up on HackQuest using the temp email.
  3. Complete email verification.
  4. Finish onboarding and access dashboard.
  5. Observe that the account is fully functional without restriction.
