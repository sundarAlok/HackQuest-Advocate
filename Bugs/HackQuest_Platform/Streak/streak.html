Daily Streak Reward Bug

Reported On: 03/01/2026

Bug Summary

When claiming the Daily Streak reward, the number of points credited to the account is inconsistent and often incorrect.

  • Displayed reward: 10 points
  • Actual points added vary between 5, 10, or 15
  • This causes total balance inconsistency

Observed Behavior

Reward Mismatch UI / Backend Desync Logic Error
Scenario Points Before Points After Points Added Status
Expected 3210 3220 +10 Correct
Observed 3210 3225 +15 Incorrect

Frequency Distribution

  • ~70% of the time → +10 points (correct)
  • ~20–25% of the time → +15 points
  • ~5–10% of the time → +5 points

This indicates a non-deterministic reward calculation, likely caused by duplicated triggers or race conditions.

Steps to Reproduce

  1. Maintain a daily streak up to 10 days.
  2. Check current point balance daily before claiming Daily Streak reward.
  3. Click Claim on the Daily Streak reward.
  4. Observe the updated balance.
  5. Compare added points with displayed reward.
